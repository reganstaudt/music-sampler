Luke Bryan

Luke Bryan is one of the most famous country artists of all time. His first ten albums had over 30 number-one hits. He is one of the world's best-selling music artist with over 75 million records sold. His debut single made it top-5 on the charts and since then he has never looked back. His music allows the listener to be transported to the place that Bryan is when singing. Whether on a beach, in a truck, or on a boat the upbeat music gets everyone dancing. Not only is Bryan a successful country artist, he has since become a judge on American Idol. He has hosted the ACM and CMA awards. Bryan helped put country music on the map and contiunes to give life to the ever growing genre.
